SECTION

This is a section. It is a logical portion of the web page. Everything within the section logically belongs together. usually a section will contain a section header in addition to its content.

</section>

Headings

This is a heading. Specifically this is an h3 heading. Headings range from h1 to h6. H1 is the most important heading of the page. There should only be a single h1 on a page, and that is usually reserved for the page title. h2 is generally used for section titles. While h3 through h6 are used for articles and other headings of less importance.
